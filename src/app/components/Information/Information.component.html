The application supports many
aspects of daily organization!

Are you asking what aspects?

Let's find out!

To-Do List

The basic application option is to create a list of duties. Each user will have the option to manage their lists, unchecking the duties performed and assigning specific lists to a given day.

Calendar

Create your own task calendar, add short notes or information about appointments. Each user is assigned the possibility of attaching additional application elements to the day, i.e. a list things to-do, shopping list, daily workout plans or adding a previously planned meal to the recipe day.

Shopping List

Have a shopping list with you always. Plan shopping for the future add it to a specific day, month or even year and never again you will lose cards or you won't forget anything important.

Recipes

Create your recipe list. Each profile will have a bookmark culinary instructions and inspiration where other users through sharing will be able to watch your culinary conquests. In simple the way you will not have zagwozdki what to cook today because the application gives you the opportunity to add a recipe to a specific day.

Training

Is it hard to keep fit? Do you often forget about daily workouts? Plan your training, add it to your planned day, and we'll tell you about itwe will remind you.
